# Privacy Policy for Focus - Website Blocker

**Last updated: January 11, 2026**

## Overview

Focus - Website Blocker ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains our data practices.

## Data Collection

**We do NOT collect, transmit, store, or share any personal information.**

The Extension operates entirely locally on your device. All data stays on your computer.

## Local Data Storage

The Extension stores the following information **locally on your device** using Chrome's built-in storage API:

- **Blocked Keywords List**: The websites/keywords you choose to block
- **Blocking Status**: Whether blocking is currently enabled or disabled

This data:
- ✅ Stays on your device only
- ✅ Is never transmitted over the internet
- ✅ Is never sent to any servers
- ✅ Is never shared with third parties
- ✅ Can be deleted by removing the extension

## Permissions

The Extension requires the following Chrome permissions:

### `storage`
**Purpose**: To save your blocked sites list and preferences locally on your device.

**What we do with it**: Store your settings in Chrome's local storage so they persist across browser sessions.

**What we DON'T do**: We do not sync this data to any cloud services or external servers.

## Third-Party Services

The Extension does NOT:
- Use analytics services
- Use tracking scripts
- Send data to external servers
- Use cookies
- Display advertisements
- Communicate with any external websites or services

## Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted, your data security is maintained by Chrome's built-in security features.

## Children's Privacy

The Extension does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 or anyone else. It does not collect data at all.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in the extension's GitHub repository and Chrome Web Store listing.

## Your Rights

You can:
- **View your data**: Open the extension popup to see your blocked keywords list
- **Delete your data**: Remove the extension from Chrome to delete all stored data
- **Export your data**: Manually copy your blocked keywords from the extension popup
